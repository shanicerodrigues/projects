Homelessness in LA: A Demonstration of Analytical Methods

Demonstration of analytical techniques of homeless communities in Los Angeles.

     Homelessness is prominant in the county of Los Angeles. In order to develop plans to reduce the number of people experiencing homelessness, we need to look at various factors. For instance, where are do the highest densities (or hotspots) of homelessness take place? What are prominant characteristics of homeless communities, do we need more attention to mental health or employment? These meaningful questions can be answered through a variety of analytical techniques as shown by this map poster (zoom in for a closer look!).

     Data sources for this project include ESRI, the Los Angeles Homeless Services Authority, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department, the Los Angeles County GIS Data Portal, and DataLA.
