About Me

     With my interests in research and environmental science, I pursued a B.Sc (Hons) in Environmental Biology with a B.Sc Minor in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Environmental Analysis at the University of Guelph in 2016 and graduated 2020. Following my bachelors degree, I wanted to specialize in spatial analysis and I began a 2.5-year program in the Master of Spatial Analysis at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson University) in 2020 and have graduated in 2023. This website will be an ongoing documentation of my experiences and projects in the world of spatial and data analysis.

Professional Experience and Education

Master of Spatial Analysis

Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson University)
(2020-2023)

For my thesis titled "Isotope-Based Calibration to Inform Water Quality in Hydrological Modelling" I simulated streamflow and water quality from southern Ontario agricultural catchments utilizing a hydrological model. The focus of the study was to assess water quality simulations informed with additional water isotope data to determine if model predictions were improved.
