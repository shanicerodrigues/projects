Public Transport Access

Bivariate mapping of areas in need of bus stops based on demographics in Chattanooga, Tennessee.

     Bivariate thematic maps showcasing demographic variables of population density against access to a vehicle and poverty to aid development decisions for future bus stops in Chattanooga, Tennessee. Hatched out regions are areas within 10 minutes walking distance of current bus stops.
